About FENDER

Fender Musical Instruments Corporation is an American company that manufactures guitars, basses, amplifiers and drums. The company was founded in 1946 by Leo Fender, and is best known for its iconic Stratocaster and Telecaster guitars.

Fender produces a wide range of guitars, from beginner guitars to high-end professional guitars. The company is also known for its innovation, and has developed many technologies over the years that have become standard in the guitar industry.

Popular Guitar Models

  • Fender Stratocaster

    The world's most popular electric guitar. Known for its versatile sound and comfortable playability.

  • Fender Telecaster

    An electric guitar with a unique and classic sound. Known for its durability and ease of playing.

  • Fender Acoustic

    Fender also produces a wide range of high-quality acoustic guitars.
