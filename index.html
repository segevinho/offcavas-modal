What is a guitar?

A guitar is a stringed instrument with a neck and a body. It is played by strumming or plucking the strings with one hand, while the other hand frets the strings on the neck to produce different notes.

There are many different types of guitars, including acoustic, electric, and bass guitars. Acoustic guitars are the most common type of guitar. They have a hollow body that produces a natural sound.

Electric guitars have a solid body and use pickups to amplify the sound of the strings. Bass guitars are similar to electric guitars, but they have four strings instead of six and are tuned lower.
