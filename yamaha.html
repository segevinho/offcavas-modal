About Yamaha

Yamaha Corporation is a Japanese multinational conglomerate headquartered in Hamamatsu, Shizuoka, Japan. It is one of the world's largest musical instrument manufacturers, and produces a wide range of products, including guitars, keyboards, drums, and digital audio workstations.

Yamaha was founded in 1887 by Torakusu Yamaha, and originally manufactured reed organs. The company expanded into the production of pianos in 1900, and began manufacturing guitars in 1942.

Yamaha is known for its high-quality, innovative products. The company has a long history of research and development, and has been responsible for many technological advancements in the music industry.
