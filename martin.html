About Martin

C. F. Martin & Company, often referred to simply as Martin, is an American manufacturer of acoustic guitars. Founded in 1833 by Christian Frederick Martin, Sr., the company is known for its high-quality, handcrafted guitars.

Martin produces a wide range of guitars, from beginner guitars to high-end professional guitars. The company is also known for its innovation, and has developed many technologies over the years that have become standard in the guitar industry.

Popular Guitar Models

  • Martin D-28

    One of the most popular acoustic guitars in the world. Known for its big, bold sound and comfortable body.

  • Martin OM-28

    A smaller-bodied acoustic guitar with a balanced sound. Known for its versatility and playability.

  • Martin 000-28

    A compact acoustic guitar with a sweet, mellow sound. Known for its fingerstyle playing.
