About Gibson

Gibson Guitar Corporation is an American manufacturer of guitars, basses, and other musical instruments. Founded in 1894 by Orville Gibson, the company is best known for its iconic Les Paul and SG guitars.

Gibson produces a wide range of guitars, from beginner guitars to high-end professional guitars. The company is also known for its innovation, and has developed many technologies over the years that have become standard in the guitar industry.

Popular Guitar Models

  • Gibson Les Paul

    One of the most popular electric guitars in the world. Known for its rich, warm sound and sustain.

  • Gibson SG

    An electric guitar with a bright, cutting sound. Known for its lightweight and comfortable body.

  • Gibson Hummingbird

    A popular acoustic guitar known for its big, bold sound.
